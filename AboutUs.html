Our Mission

Bytewave was born from the vision of three friends who believed high-quality PCs should be accessible to everyone. Frustrated with overpriced, unmodifiable systems dominating the market, they set up a makeshift workshop in a small garage, relying on passion and creativity to bring their ideas to life. Their first creation, a compact modular PC, was far from perfect but marked the beginning of their mission to revolutionize personal computing.

2022

Encouraged by early success in 2021, the team refined their designs based on user feedback and launched a crowdfunding campaign that exceeded expectations by 1,000%. This overwhelming support allowed them to scale up operations, invest in advanced tools, and hire a team of like-minded professionals. By the end of the year, Bytewave had sold its first batch of systems, earning a reputation as a promising new player in the custom PC market.

2023

In 2023, Bytewave unveiled its flagship product, the Modulus, at a major tech expo. This modular PC, designed for effortless upgrades and customization, bridged the gap between performance and accessibility. It was hailed as a game-changer by industry experts and customers alike, leading to strategic partnerships and global distribution. 2024

By 2024, Bytewave expanded its product offerings with the EcoPC, an energy-efficient model for environmentally conscious users, and an AI-powered software suite for real-time performance optimization. Committed to sustainability, they introduced eco-friendly packaging and minimized production waste. The opening of their flagship store in June provided a hands-on space for customers to explore their innovations, participate in workshops, and build custom PCs with expert guidance.
